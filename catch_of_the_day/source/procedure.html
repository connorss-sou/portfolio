Location Largest Fish

Author: Sean Connors

Description:

This page allows you to view the largest fish caught at the selected location.



Justification:

This page is useful for all users who may be curious how large the fish get at certain locations. This is something that as a potential user of a system like this would use to scout out new areas I'd like to fish.


The Rogue River, or Depot Bay are good options for this because they have a few catches to pick between. If the rogue river river is picked, the result should be a 21lb Chinook Salmon, since this is the largest fish in the catch records. If nothing is returned, this is because not all locations have a catch record, so there are no fish to return. Just go back and choose another location.
